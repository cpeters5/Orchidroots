OrchidRoots V.3.1 - 2020-10-01
User Accounts
  • Authentication: User must sign in with unique user id and email. A confirmation email will be sent after a successful registration or after an email change request.
  • Password reset: User can reset password
  • Socialmedia login: User can login with active Facebook account.

Navigation Bar
The dropdown list of genera, species and hybrids were removed from the navbar and put in a new page "Detailed Search". This helps improve page load speed significantly.

Three Access levels
  • Basic view: Anonymous Users can access public pages, search, browse images and detail pages.
  • Private view: Registered Users can access all features in the basic level including ability to upload and manage their own photos in their private album. Registered users can also view filtered lists of species and hybrids.
  • Curate view: Registered Curators can access all features in the private level including the ability to approve or reject private photos. Curators can also reidentify a miss-identified photo and update information related to a species of hybrid.

Detail Page
  • Summary tab: This page shows a short selection of public photos that best represents the defining characteristics of the orchid. The page includes a summary of taxonomy, and other information related to the orchid. Photos of parents and species ancestors are also shown for hybrid.
  • Photo tab: This view combines public and private photos, where private photos are visible to their owner only. This simplifies navigation and allows users to view all public and private photos in the same page. A number of control buttons in this view allow registered users to select species or hybrid containing their photos. Registered curators will see a different sets of control buttons that allow them to manage photos such as approving, setting status, or reidentifing.
  • Compare tab: Allow users to compare two species side by side and create a new hybrid if the cross between the two orchids does not exist in OrchidRoots.

Donate function
We have registered for a donate function on Paypal to make it convenient for users to help with our cause. Please consider making a donation today... thank you!
